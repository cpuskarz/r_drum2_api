Neil Peart

Neil Ellwood Peart, born September 12, 1952), is a Canadian-American musician and author.

Best known as the drummer and primary lyricist for the rock band Rush.
Peart has received numerous awards for his musical performances,
including an induction into the Modern Drummer Hall of Fame in 1983,
making him the youngest person ever so honored.

He is known for his technical proficiency and stamina.
