Steve Gadd

Stephen Kendall, a.k.a: Steve Gadd (born April 9, 1945) is an American musician.


Gadd is one of the most well-known and highly regarded session and studio drummers
in the industry,recognized by his induction into the Modern Drummer Hall of Fame in 1984.
Know for his impeccable ability to lay a groove "in the pocket".

Gadd's groove on Paul Simon's "50 Ways to Leave Your Lover", and drum solo on Steely Dan's "Aja",
are both examples of his seminal sound and signature style. He has worked with popular musicians
from a wide range of genres.
